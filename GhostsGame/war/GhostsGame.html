----- Ghosts Game Rules -----

Each player has four white piece and four evil piece at the beginning

Deploy Phase:

  • Firstly both White and Black player need to deploy pieces on board
  • You can only deploy on two rows that is on your side except for exit

Move Phase:

  • Each round you can move one piece one step
  • You can capture opponent's any piece

Winning Condition:

  • Capture opponent's four white piece
  • Or move one white piece to exit on opponent side
